# WBP-Weighted-Scoring-Framework
Interactive amenity weight and equity multiplier simulator. Adjust sliders to explore how scoring priorities affect community rankings.
The simulator has five real Bay Area community profiles built in, each with plausible amenity mixes based on what you know about those places. As you move the sliders, the scores update live and re-rank. Notice how Napa Oxbow and Suisun City consistently move up when you raise the EPC multiplier, and how Healdsburg's score drops sharply when you lower the luxury grocery weight, since that's its primary amenity signal.
The "research priorities flagged" panel at the bottom surfaces which weight choices have thin evidence bases. The two biggest open questions this framework currently cannot answer without more literature are the coworking/third-place café weight (the post-pandemic VMT evidence for suburban coworking specifically is thin) and the luxury grocery calibration (whether a Whole Foods generates local walking trips or primarily car trips from a regional catchment is an empirical question that likely varies by context).
